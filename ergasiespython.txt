In the years after the Civil War, Jo March lives in New York City and makes her living as a writer, while her sister Amy March  studies painting in Paris. Amy has a chance encounter with Theodore "Laurie" Laurence , a childhood crush who proposed to Jo, but was ultimately rejected. Their oldest sibling, Meg March (Emma Watson), is married to a schoolteacher, while shy sister Beth (Eliza Scanlen) develops a devastating illness that brings the family back together.
